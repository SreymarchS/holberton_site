About Me

Hi, I’m March! I’m an aspiring software developer transitioning from a registered nurse. I’ve always been fascinated by technology, and I’m now pursuing my dream of becoming a developer. I have completed Certificate III in Information Technology, where I’m learning software development through hands-on projects and peer collaboration. I’m an adventurous and enthusiastic person, always seeking new challenges and eager to expand my skill set. This passion for learning and exploring new opportunities is what drives my transition into software development, as I believe it’s the perfect field for me to grow, innovate, and continue my journey of discovery.

Learning and Skills

    Languages: Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS

    Tools & Frameworks: Git, Node.js, React (currently learning), Express

    Problem Solving & Critical Thinking: You ensured precise patient care, similar to writing clean, bug-free code and conducting thorough testing in development.

    Attention to detail: As a nurse, you made quick decisions to ensure patient safety, which translates into troubleshooting and debugging code in software development.

    Team Collaboration & Communication: Working with medical teams has prepared you for collaborating effectively with developers, designers, and PMs in tech environments.

    Adapting to New Technologies: Just as you adapted to new medical equipment, you’ll quickly learn new programming languages and technologies in development.

    User-Centered Mindset: Prioritizing patient comfort and care translates into designing user-friendly software that meets the needs of end-users.

My Journey: Aspiring to Join Holberton, PEXA, and SHEE

    Why I Chose Software Development:
    I’m passionate about technology and how it can solve real-world problems. As a scrub/scout nurse, I developed a deep appreciation for precision, organization, and problem-solving. Whether I was preparing instruments for a surgical procedure or assisting the surgeon, I learned how important it is to be detail-oriented and proactive. These skills have naturally translated into my journey toward software development, where the same focus on collaboration, planning, and execution is crucial. I thrive in environments where I am continuously learning and developing new skills. My background in healthcare, coupled with my passion for technology, motivates me to bring a unique perspective to the tech world.

    Why Holberton School:
    I chose Holberton School because of its unique, hands-on, project-based curriculum that emphasizes learning through real-world applications. Holberton’s peer-driven approach and focus on collaboration are incredibly appealing to me because I believe that learning from others and solving complex problems together is the best way to prepare for a career in tech. I am currently developing my skills in software engineering, and I’m excited to continue growing as a developer.

    Why PEXA?
    I am deeply excited about the prospect of working at PEXA, a company that is transforming the property settlement process. PEXA’s innovative approach to creating digital property transactions resonates with my passion for technology and efficiency. While I’m still early in my career, I’m eager to learn from PEXA’s forward-thinking team and contribute my skills to their mission of simplifying and improving the real estate industry through technology.

    Why the SHEE Program?
    Being a woman in tech, I am passionate about empowering others and promoting diversity in the STEM field. The SHEE Program is something I aspire to be part of because it focuses on supporting women in engineering and entrepreneurship. I believe that diversity is key to driving innovation, and the SHEE Program offers a platform for women to grow, lead, and mentor others in the tech industry. By joining this program, I hope to continue building my skills and contribute to fostering a more inclusive tech community.

My Future Goals

    Develop innovative software solutions: I want to build applications that help solve real-world challenges, particularly in the real estate sector.

    Contribute to PEXA’s digital transformation: I aspire to contribute to PEXA’s mission of transforming property transactions through technology and innovation.

    Leadership in tech and mentoring: I aim to lead by example, inspiring and mentoring the next generation of developers, particularly women in STEM.

    Entrepreneurship in tech: I dream of one day launching my own tech startup, combining my healthcare background and software development expertise to create innovative solutions.
