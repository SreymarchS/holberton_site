About Me

Hi, I’m March! I’m an aspiring software developer transitioning from a registered nurse. I’ve always been fascinated by technology, and I’m now pursuing my dream of becoming a developer. I have completed Certificate III in Information technology, where I’m learning software development through hands-on projects and peer collaboration.

Learning and Skills

  • Languages: Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS
  • Tools & Frameworks: Git, Node.js, React (Currently learning)
  • Soft Skills: Problem-solving, collaboration, critical thinking, time management

Why Holberton School?

I chose Holberton School because of its project-based, peer-driven approach. I love the challenge of learning by doing and working with others to solve real-world problems. The school’s emphasis on building a strong foundation in computer science is exactly what I need to succeed as a developer.

What I’m Working On

While I’m still in the early stages of my journey, I’ve worked on some foundational projects like building simple websites and learning the basics of web development. I’m continuously learning and improving my skills, and I’m excited to start working on more complex projects in the near future.
